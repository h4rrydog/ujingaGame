Use of cookies

We use Google Analytics and Google Analytics Advertising Features on this website to find out how many people are accessing our website, which content is most popular, how people find our website and where in the world they access our website from, and anonymous demographic and interest information about our visitors.

You can find out more information about Google Analytics and the cookies it uses here: https://developers.google.com/analytics/devguides/collection/analyticsjs/cookie-usage

To understand more about cookies, and how to delete them from your computer, there are many helpful resources on the internet including this one: http://www.allaboutcookies.org/manage-cookies/clear-cookies-installed.html

And you can opt-out of Google Analytics Advertising Features by following this link.

Web-forms

Where we have web-forms within our website inviting viewers to contact us for a variety of reasons, the information is gathered and stored via MailChimp. By completing and submitting information via a web-form you are granting permission for this information to be stored in our Mailchimp account, and used by us to contact you directly or via our occasional newsletters.

To contact us without using the web-forms, please email us at hello@helixcentre.com

We will not pass contact information gathered through our website onto any third parties.

Newsletters

Our occasional emailed newsletters are sent via Mailchimp, and each one offers you the opportunity to unsubscribe. You can also contact us directly to be removed from our mailing list at hello@helixcentre.com
