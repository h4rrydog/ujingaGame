Make your friends do silly things.

Ujinga is the ridiculous activity cards game where you make your friends act out silly things.

You’re the Ringmaster. Choose a three card challenge for your friends to combine and act out at the same time. Make it silly. Make it hard. Make them sweat.

Watch your friends perform in order to win your challenge. Choose the funniest act. But don’t be too mean! What goes around comes around.
